The story of Łódź wiOSłuje

The story begins when Kamila went to Kraków in November 2013 to participate in a CocoaHeads meetup. Paweł Dudek, who was one of the speaker, suggested that Łódź is missing an iOS community. A bit reluctant and without realising that she is about to start something awsome, Kamila agreed to organise the first meetup on the 3rd of December 2013. From that point on, Paweł Dudek is known as the godfather of the group.

Since then meetings are held regularly and Łódź wiOSłuje became the best place for iOS developers in Łódź. The group owes its brilliant name to its second godfather, Marek Defeciński, president of JUG Łódź.
